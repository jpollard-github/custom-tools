# Google Form

## Recommended Form Title

Business Workflow Intake

## Recommended Description

Tell us how your current process works, where it breaks down, and what outcome you want. We use this to recommend a better internal workflow or tool.

## Fields

Use these as long-answer unless otherwise noted.

1. Business name
2. Contact name
3. Contact email
4. Industry
5. Company size
   - multiple choice:
   - `1-5`
   - `6-20`
   - `21-50`
   - `51+`
6. What process are you trying to improve?
7. Describe the current step-by-step workflow.
8. What usually starts this process?
9. Who touches this process?
10. What information gets created or updated?
11. Where does the process slow down or break?
12. What gets tracked manually today?
13. What tools are currently involved?
   - checkbox:
   - Gmail
   - Google Sheets
   - Excel
   - QuickBooks
   - CRM
   - Slack
   - Phone calls
   - Other
14. Approximate volume
   - short answer example:
   - `15 requests/week`
15. What outcome do you want instead?
16. What reports or visibility would help most?
17. Who needs access to the new system?
18. Is this customer-facing, staff-facing, or both?
19. Timeline or urgency
20. Anything else we should know?

## Hidden/Internal Columns To Add In The Sheet

After the Form feeds the Sheet, add these columns manually:

- `lead_status`
- `qualified`
- `summary_generated`
- `spec_generated`
- `proposal_sent`
- `estimated_build_size`
- `notes`

## What Good Responses Look Like

Good responses usually mention:

- where requests originate
- who does manual handoffs
- where data is duplicated
- where follow-up is delayed
- what the owner wants visibility into
